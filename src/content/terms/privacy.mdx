Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services through the service web application (“Service”), which allows users to manage and post content to TikTok.

By using service, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

**1\. Information We Collect**
------------------------------

We collect only the data required to provide our services, including:

### **a. TikTok Account Information**

When you connect your TikTok account via OAuth, we collect:

*   Basic profile information (e.g., username, profile image)
    
*   Access tokens required to post content on your behalf
    
*   Video-related metadata (titles, captions, hashtags)
    

### **b. User-Submitted Content**

We collect:

*   Video files you upload
    
*   Captions and other media metadata
    
*   Scheduled post times
    

### **c. Usage Data**

We may automatically collect:

*   IP address and device/browser information
    
*   Timestamps of actions within the platform
    
*   Analytics data for improving service performance
    

**2\. How We Use Your Information**
-----------------------------------

We use your data to:

*   Authenticate and connect your TikTok account
    
*   Schedule and post videos to TikTok on your behalf
    
*   Provide account management and history features
    
*   Improve our product functionality and user experience
    
*   Monitor and prevent abuse of our systems
    

**3\. How We Share Your Information**
-------------------------------------

We **do not sell** your personal information to third parties.

We only share your data with:

*   **TikTok**, for posting content through their API
    
*   **Service providers**, strictly for infrastructure or hosting purposes
    
*   **Law enforcement**, if legally required
    

**4\. Data Retention**
----------------------

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

*   OAuth tokens and video metadata are stored until you revoke access or delete your account
    
*   Scheduled videos and posts are deleted after successful posting or manual deletion
    
*   Analytics data is stored in aggregate, anonymized form
    

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

**5\. Security**
----------------

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no online platform is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

*   Access to your data
    
*   Correction or deletion of your data
    
*   Revocation of consent

To exercise your rights, contact us at **privacy@service.app**.
